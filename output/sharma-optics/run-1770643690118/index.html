The Visionary Heritage

Founded by Atin Sharma in 1999, Sharma Optics was built on a singular promise: to bring world-class eye care and exclusive global fashion to the heart of Indore.

Over the last 25 years, we have served over 50,000 happy clients on MG Road, evolving from a local boutique into Indore’s most trusted name for luxury optics. Our commitment to quality remains as unwavering today as it was on our first morning.
